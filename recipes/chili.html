Turkey Chilli Recipe

Low Fat Pan Made Chilli

This high protein chili recipe is packed with flavor, healthy, and the best part?

Doesn’t take all day to make! You don’t need a slow cooker to make this and you can make enough for the whole week!

Ingredients

  • 1.5 Tablespoons Olive Oil
  • 1.3 lbs Lean Ground Beef or Turkey
  • 16 oz Water
  • 1/2 Red Onion (chopped)
  • 1 Red Pepper
  • 1.5 Teaspoons Minced Garlic
  • 6 Tablespoons Green Chiles
  • 28 oz Crushed Tomatoes
  • 1 Teaspoon Crushed Red Pepper
  • 15 oz Black Beans
  • 16 oz Dark Red Kidney Beans
  • 1 Packet Chili Seasoning Mix (1.25 oz)

Preparation Steps

  1. Take out a large stovetop pan and turn your burner on Medium Heat
  2. Add Olive Oil and let heat
  3. Cook Ground Beef or Turkey in pan until brown
  4. Add the the rest of the ingredients while mixing
  5. Bring your chili to a boil, cover, turn your burner on Low Heat, and let your chili simmer for 30-35 minutes
