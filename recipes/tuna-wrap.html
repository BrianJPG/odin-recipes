Tuna Avacado Wrap Recipe

Low Carb Tuna Salad with Avacado

If you’re looking for a quick, low carb, and healthy meal idea… you’re going to love these Tuna Avocado Lettuce Wraps! This keto friendly lunch recipe has under 5 grams of carbs per wrap, takes around 2 minutes to prep, and comes packed with protein!

Ingredients

  • 2 Cans Tuna - Drained
  • 2.65 Ounces Plain Greek Yogurt
  • ½ Teaspoon Italian Seasoning
  • ½ Teaspoon Lemon Juice
  • ½ Cup Cheddar Cheese
  • ½ Avocado
  • 1 Pinch Black Pepper
  • Lettuce

Preparation Steps

  1. Add all of your ingredients into a bowl
  2. Mix everything together until the Avocado is mashed in (use the back of a fork to mash it easier)
  3. Take out a piece of lettuce, put some of your mix into it, and wrap it up
